Pickup Guidelines

Preparing trash properly for recycling pickup is crucial to ensure materials can be effectively processed. Here are some key guidelines to follow:

  • Clean and Rinse Items: Remove Residue, Dry Completely
  • Separate Different Materials: Remove Mixed Materials(e.g., plastic and metal in a single product)
  • Flatten Cardboard and Plastic Bottles: Break Down Boxes, Squash Bottles
  • Remove Non-Recyclable Components: Caps and Labels, Non-Recyclable Extras
  • Do Not Include Hazardous Materials: Special Disposal for Hazardous waste, Dispose of Medical Waste Separately.
  • Prepare E-Waste Properly: Remove Batteries, Protect Fragile Items.
  • Bundle or Contain Small Items: Group Small Pieces Together.
  • Check for Specific Local Requirements: Local Recycling

    • Instructions: Always review guidelines from your local recycling center as rules vary by location, especially for uncommon items (like aerosols or certain plastics)
